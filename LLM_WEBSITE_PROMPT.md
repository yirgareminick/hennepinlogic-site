## Technical Requirements & Integration

**Phone System Compatibility:**
- Works via call forwarding or SIP trunk
- Compatible with Verizon, AT&T, local carriers, and VoIP systems
- Customer's phone number stays the same
- Configurable: can handle after-hours only, overflow, or 24/7

**Lead Data Management:**

**Emergency Escalation:**
- Keyword detection for urgent situations

## Service Tier Structure

The business offers three tiers that allow businesses to choose their level of automation based on their needs, comfort level, and operational requirements. These should be prominently featured as the core service differentiation:

### Tier 1: Missed Call Coverage

**What It Does:**
- Monitors phone lines for missed or unanswered calls
- Sends immediate automated SMS response when a call goes unanswered
- Captures basic lead information through text conversation
- Logs all interactions in the dashboard

**Ideal For:**
- Businesses with existing reception staff who handle most calls
- Companies that only need backup coverage during busy periods
- Businesses wanting to test automation with minimal disruption
- Small operations with occasional missed calls

**Key Value:**
- Entry-level option that fills gaps in existing coverage
- Ensures no lead is completely ignored
- Lower cost point for businesses testing the waters
- Works alongside current call handling processes

### Tier 2: Hybrid Reception

**What It Does:**
- Automated system handles initial response to all calls and web forms
- AI conducts preliminary qualification and information gathering
- Escalates complex situations or specific criteria to human staff
- Combines speed of automation with human oversight when needed
- Configurable rules for when to engage human team members

**Ideal For:**
- Businesses wanting to maintain personal touch for certain situations
- Companies with part-time reception or small teams
- Operations that get high volume but want human validation
- Businesses in transition toward fuller automation

**Key Value:**
- Best of both worlds: speed + human judgment
- Reduces staff workload while maintaining quality control
- Customizable escalation rules based on business priorities
- Smooth transition path between manual and automated operations

### Tier 3: Full AI Coverage

**What It Does:**
- Complete automated handling of all inbound communications (calls, texts, web forms)
- Conducts full qualification conversations autonomously
- Schedules appointments and dispatches based on urgency
- Handles follow-up sequences without human intervention
- Emergency escalation to on-call staff when critical keywords detected
- 24/7 operation with no human oversight required

**Ideal For:**
- Businesses ready to fully automate lead response
- After-hours and weekend coverage needs
- High-volume operations that can't staff adequately
- Companies prioritizing speed and consistency over personal touch
- Businesses with well-defined processes that can be automated

**Key Value:**
- Maximum speed and consistency in responses
- True 24/7 coverage without staffing costs
- Scales effortlessly with lead volume increases
- Frees team to focus entirely on service delivery
- Highest ROI for businesses with predictable lead patterns

### Tier Comparison

| Feature | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 |
|---------|--------|--------|--------|
| Missed call response | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Web form response | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| Initial qualification | Basic | Advanced | Complete |
| Human escalation | Manual | Rule-based | Emergency only |
| 24/7 coverage | Text only | Configurable | Full |
| Follow-up sequences | — | Optional | Automatic |
| Staff involvement | High | Medium | Minimal |

**Implementation Note:** Most businesses start with Tier 1 or 2 and scale up as they gain confidence in the system and understand their specific needs.
